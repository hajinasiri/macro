Map Objects

Map objects are hierarchically organized and can be opened/closed to show/hide their 'child-objects'.

on an object to open/close that object.
on an object to open/close all children of that object.

When you hover or click on certain map objects, additional information about that object will appear in a tooltip and/or the 'info-pane'.

Panes

You can size the 'story-pane' (on the left or East) or the 'info-pane' (on the right or West), by dragging the dividers. The panes can also be toggled open/closed by clicking-on the dividers or by using the 'w' or 'e' keys:

• Highlighting

• Zooming

• Tracing

• Editing
