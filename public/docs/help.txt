        <h4>Map Objects</h4>
        <p>
            Map objects are hierarchically organized and can be opened/closed to show/hide their 'child-objects'.
            <br>
            <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                <tbody>
                    <tr>
                        <td width="100"><img src="/docs/svg/open.svg" alt="open" height="25"></td>
                        <td>on an object to open/close that object.</td>
                    </tr>
                    <tr>
                        <td><img src="/docs/svg/opt-click.svg" alt="option-click" height="25"></td>
                        <td>on an object to open/close all children of that object.</td>
                    </tr>
                </tbody>
            </table>
            <p>
                When you hover or click on certain map objects, additional information about that object will appear in a tooltip and/or the 'info-pane'.
            </p>
            <h4>Panes</h4>
            <p>You can size the 'story-pane' (on the left or East) or the 'info-pane' (on the right or West), by dragging the dividers. The panes can also be toggled open/closed by clicking-on the dividers or by using the 'w' or 'e' keys:
                <br>
                <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                    <tbody>
                        <tr>
                            <td width="100"><img src="/docs/svg/panes.svg" alt="panes" height="25"></td>
                            <td>to toggle the 'west' (story) or 'east' (info) panes open/closed.</td>
                        </tr>
                    </tbody>
                </table>
                <h4>Pan & Zoom</h4>
                <p>Mouse and trackpad panning and zooming should work as usual. Or you can use these keys:</p>
                <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                    <tbody>
                        <tr>
                            <td width="100"><img src="/docs/svg/zoom.svg" alt="zoom" height="25"></td>
                            <td>to zoom out / in / all </td>
                        </tr>
                        <tr>
                            <td><img src="/docs/svg/arrows.svg" alt="arrows" height="55"></td>
                            <td>to pan.</td>
                        </tr>
                        <tr>
                            <td><img src="/docs/svg/g-click.svg" alt="g-click" height="25"></td>
                            <td>on an object, to open and zoom-in on that object.</td>
                        </tr>
                        <tr>
                            <td><img src="/docs/svg/url.svg" alt="shift-click" height="25"></td>
                            <td>anywhere in the map (but not on an object), to set the URL to the current view.</td>
                        </tr>
                    </tbody>
                </table>
                <h4>Changing Perspectives</h4>
                <p>'Perspectives' provide a set of pre-defined views of the map. To move through the perspectives:
                    <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                        <tbody>
                            <tr>
                                <td width="215"><img src="/docs/svg/slides.svg" alt="slides" height="25"></td>
                                <td>move forward / backward through the different perspectives.</td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td><img src="/docs/svg/slides2.svg" alt="slides2" height="25"></td>
                                <td>for the first/last perspective.</td>
                            </tr>
                        </tbody>
                    </table>
                    <h4>Animations</h4>
                    <p>Once an animation is playing, it can be controlled with:
                        <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                            <tbody>
                                <tr>
                                    <td width="100"><img src="/docs/svg/space.svg" alt="space" height="25"></td>
                                    <td>to pause/resume.</td>
                                </tr>
                                <tr>
                                    <td><img src="/docs/svg/r-f.svg" alt="r-f" height="25"></td>
                                    <td>to skip reverse / forward one segment of the animation.</td>
                                </tr>
                            </tbody>
                        </table>
                        <h4>Printing</h4>
                        <table width=auto style="margin-left: 20px;">
                            <tbody>
                                <tr>
                                    <td width="100"><img src="/docs/svg/print.svg" alt="print" height="25"></td>
